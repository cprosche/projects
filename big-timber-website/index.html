Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the minimum age?

The minimum age for throwing is 13 years old, however we allow as young as 10 to come as spectators only!

How do I book?

You can book online here or by messaging or calling us directly at 815-262-3399

What should I wear?

Close toe shoes are required. We recommend loose fit clothing so you’re comfortable throwing.
